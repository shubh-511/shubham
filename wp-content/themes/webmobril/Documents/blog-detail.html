Never Fall for Free Web Hosting Service!

Getting a web hosting service free of cost sounds a mind shattering deal, right?

Maybe, free web hosting not persist as you are expecting it to be. Before we get into why a website owner should not fall for free web hosting? Let’s know a bit about what it is actually.

A Free web hosting offers nothing more than a storage space for your website and a website builder with limited plugins and no security feature. It is definitely not a preferred and secure option for a business website.

Why you should not:

  • The appearance of the website and functionality gets restricted to minimal. Owner don’t get much variety of designs to compare and choose the best for the business or blog.
  • Ads on free hosted sites are one thing that visitors find irritating and resist user to use your services over and over again. On a paid hosting service you need not to deal with ads.
  • Your host will not be responsible for any kind of miscellaneous activities on your website. By subscribing a free hosting service you opt for complete backend control over your website, which includes server, files, and security.
  • Zero security measure are provided with a free hosting plan. Probably you will get a shared server, the favourite target of hackers because their layers are easy to penetrate.

Hiring a paid web hosting plan from a reliable service provider helps you eradicate from boundary limitations of creativity, management and security. They take the accountability of full backend support and up time of the website. Hosting companies offer numerous plans covering wide and decent cost range. Don’t cage yourself in trap of free service. List your requirements, research and go ahead with the best plan that meets them.
