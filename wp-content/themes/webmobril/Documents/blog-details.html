Is Reseller Hosting The Right Option for Making Profits?

From a give and take inclined earning process, reseller hosting service allows the user to take data space on lease from third party data center server provider, and give (or resell) the rented hosting service underneath their own brand name. Idea of reseller hosting is everything related to making decent revenue, buying from one end and reselling to the other.

Process

It’s simple as reseller buys a large data space and bandwidth from the parent company and separates it into small packages for selling it to customers further. The resources offered in reseller hosting are:


  • 1. Hard Disk Space

  • 2. Bandwidth

  • 3. Database

  • 4. Email accounts

  • 5. Add-on Domains

  • 6. FTP Accounts



How Reseller Market is Growing?

The present-day picture of web hosting market is feasible. The hosting product such as VPS and dedicated server are available at quite affordable rate plans along with excelled specifications. It has become beneficial for a hosts to assign SHH access, creating resellers and more. Reseller hosting market is still elevating but not with previous marginal profits. Years back 20 GB of space used to cost $35 per month from reseller, which is now in line with user expects, 250 GB of space for $5 per month or even more. Despites of market updates, reselling hosts are still earning profits.

Reason to Choose Reseller Hosting

Reseller hosting is the finest if you are up to start your own web hosting service under your own brand name. It becomes easier to gather the maintenance cost of the server and eliminates high investments. It broadens the possibilities for resellers for creating and increasing their own customer base. Mostly this service is subscribed by web designers, development companies, or anyone who gets to host more than one websites can go with reseller hosting. All the technical parts are managed by the web host without any extra charges.
