Privacy Policy:
When it comes to visitors of Web page, our web server simply recognizes the domain name of customer and not the email address. We make sure that the information collected from us is used for internal view and afterwards is completely discarded, which means not shared with other organizations.

In context to Ad Servers:
We don’t have any kind of partnership with any ad sever companies. Sometimes, we may utilize customer information for new, unanticipated uses, which are not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. In case of any changes in our information practices, we post the policy regarding the changes to our site. If you are concerned about the way new information is used, there is a need to check the website periodically.

In context to Website Security :
Industry-standard encryption technologies are used at the time of transferring and receiving consumer data exchanged with the site. When there is a transfer of crucial data like health and financial information, we send visitors to a completely secure server and notify them through a pop-up screen on the website. Appropriate Website Security measures are used to protect against loss, alteration or misuse of information.

In context to cookies:
Cookies are used to record session information, which includes products that are added to the shopping cart by customers, content of web page based on visitor’s browser type or any other information. If you think that you don’t need any kind of e-mail then you can inform about this by simply emailing us.
